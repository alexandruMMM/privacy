Privacy Policy for LocalShare

Effective date: August 27, 2025

LocalShare respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share personal data from users.

How the app works

  • All file transfers happen locally over Wi-Fi or a personal hotspot.
  • No data is sent to external servers or third parties.
  • Each transfer session generates a unique local URL for security.

Permissions used

  • Wi-Fi / Hotspot access – required to create or use the local connection.
  • Storage access – required to select and transfer your files.

Data collection

LocalShare does not collect any personal data, usage data, or analytics.
