Privacy Policy — Scanova PDF Smart Document Scanner (“Scanova PDF”)

Effective Date: April 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Scanova PDF Smart Document Scanner application (“the app”) handles data when you use it.

1. Summary

  • The app is designed for scanning, editing, and exporting PDFs, with primary processing performed locally on the device.
  • The app does not require an account and, by default, does not upload your documents to any servers.

2. Data We Process

The app may process the following types of data only to provide functionality:

  • Images/documents: photos taken with the camera or images imported by you.
  • Generated files: PDFs/exports created at your request.
  • Local preferences: app settings stored locally.

We do not intentionally collect:

  • name, email, or phone number;
  • advertising identifiers;
  • location data.

3. Device Permissions

  • Camera — used to scan documents (if you choose).

Depending on Android version, the OS may show file picker dialogs for importing or saving files.

4. Sharing and Transfers

  • The app may allow you to share PDFs via email, messaging apps, etc.
  • Sharing occurs only at your request.

5. Third-Party Services

The app does not intentionally integrate analytics, ads, or trackers.

Note: Google Play and Android may collect diagnostic data under their own policies.

6. Data Security

  • Use a screen lock;
  • Keep your device updated;
  • Avoid sharing sensitive files via untrusted services.

7. Children’s Privacy

The app does not intentionally collect children’s personal data.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date.
